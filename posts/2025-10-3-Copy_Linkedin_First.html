Starting my “Blog Posts”

Hello everyone!

It’s been a while since I last posted here, but that’s about to change.

Classes started back up about a month ago, and after Expo I realized that while I spend a lot of time working on engineering projects outside of class, almost no one in my professional world gets to see them unless I share. Right now, my LinkedIn doesn’t really reflect the work I’m most proud of — and I want to change that.

So, I’m starting a weekly blog here on LinkedIn (and hopefully on my website soon). Each week, I’ll share:

  • A summary of interesting projects I’ve worked on in the last week
  • Anything currently in-progress that I hope to update more on in the future
  • A short analysis of a tech update that caught my attention

Part of this is to push myself to stay on top of current events, but it’s also about improving my communication skills and opening up my projects to a wider audience. If I’m accountable to all of you instead of just myself, I believe even more exciting things can get done.

Stay tuned to see my first post — I’m excited to hear your thoughts!
